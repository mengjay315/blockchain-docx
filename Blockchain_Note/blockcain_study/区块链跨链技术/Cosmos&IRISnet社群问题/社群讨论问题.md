**问题七【来自腾讯区块链场外论坛】**
Cosmos怎样控制ATOM的通胀呢，是不是意味着，拿着ATOM不参加Staking的话就会慢慢贬值？

ATOM inflation is a function of the percentage of total ATOMs staked at any given time, with a floor of 7% inflation. If more than 2/3 of the total ATOM supply is staked, then inflation remains low. If less than 2/3 of the total ATOM supply is staked, then inflation will steadily increase, up to a maximum of 20%, until the desired staked amount is reached. Individuals, and even large groups of individuals, who do not stake their ATOMs does not necessarily cause ATOMs to decrease in value. The value of ATOMs, like the value of every cryptocurrency at the moment, is currently reflective of speculation rather than staked percentage.

ATOM的通胀率是任意时间抵押ATOM总数百分比的函数，最低为7％。 如果抵押的ATOM超过总量的2/3，那么通胀率仍然很低。 如果少于ATOM总量的2/3，则通胀率会稳步攀升，最高达20％，直至达到所需的抵押量。 不管是个人还是更大的群体没有参与ATOM抵押也不会导致ATOM的贬值。ATOM的价值和目前每种加密货币的价值一样，大家似乎更愿意以投机的价格来衡量，而非抵押比例。

**问题十【来自TokenClub】**
质押部分的币是质押给谁呢，这部分币的用途是怎样的？Atom增发还有质押机制关系是什么？

When you stake ATOMs, you are staking on the Cosmos Hub. ATOMs are sort of like the digital analogs of GPUs, FPGAs, and ASICs used in PoW coins. In PoS, staking tokens like ATOMs are used for Sybil-resistance. This, when you stake ATOMs, you are putting up financial collateral that gets burned if the protocol detects any malicious activity. This is how PoS simulates the burning of electricity in PoW. In PoW, the financial disincentive to attack the network is the cost of expending a physical resource like electricity. Since PoS doesn't use hardware to burn electricity, it must simulate the same financial cost to attack the network by using a staking token.

是抵押在Cosmos Hub。ATOM有点像POW系统中GPUs，FPGAs和ASICs 的数模。在POS网络中，像ATOM这样的抵押通证就会用于抵抗Sybil。 这样，当您使用ATOM时，如果协议检测到任何恶意活动，您抵押的ATOM就会被消耗。 这就是PoS如何模拟PoW中的电力消耗。

在PoW中，攻击网络的因素是消耗电力等物理资源的成本。 由于PoS不使用硬件来耗电，因此必须利用taking来消耗成本

Cosmos通过创建新分区来解决网络中的扩展性问题，分区无限多时，那hub怎么保证各个分区同步更新？Cosmos可以与现存系统互操作吗？

With zones, this specifically addresses horizontal scalability. Because each zone is sovereign, this means that they can run independently and sync up asynchronously when they wish to use IBC to transact with one another. Existing systems like Ethereum and Bitcoin, which have probabilistic finality because of PoW, can't interoperate with Cosmos Hub yet without a "peg zone". We need to build peg zones, which act as adaptor chains, to finalize PoW chains on order to communicate with finalizing chains like the Cosmos Hub.
